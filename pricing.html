Pricing

Sessions are available in-person across Vancouver and the Lower Mainland, or online via Zoom. Rates range from $40–$50 per hour depending on grade level and session length.

How it works

You only pay for learning sessions

Most tutoring charges you for every hour — including time spent reviewing material you've already covered. I do things differently.

Research on spaced repetition shows that reviewing material at gradually increasing intervals is far more effective for long-term retention than cramming or back-to-back sessions. Rather than spending paid time on repetitive review, I structure lessons so that new material is introduced in paid one-on-one sessions, and revision is handled separately — for free.

Every week I host free 2–3 hour group online revision sessions, open to all current students. These are structured study blocks where you can review what we covered, work through practice problems, and ask questions in a low-pressure environment. Think of them as open office hours.
